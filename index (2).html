The Show

Catalysts for Change

What started as a project with students at Nova School of Business & Economics has grown into something much bigger. LITS X is a long-format podcast where David and his students sit down with top performers and inspiring guests to explore the issues shaping today's and tomorrow's world.

No scripts. No filters. Just honest conversations in a genuinely relaxed environment — covering strategy, technology, mental health, biohacking, genetics, leadership, and beyond.

Life Is Too Short not to make the best of it.

Hosted By

David
Bernardo
Santo

David Bernardo Santo is not your typical podcast host.

Recognised by Forbes USA as one of the top experts in ecommerce and digital strategy in Latin America, named by Entrepreneur magazine as one of the top 10 innovators in Mexico, and twice awarded Portugal's prestigious "40 Under 40."

CEO and founder of LITS Adventures, advisor to Amazon, Walmart, Sony Music, and L'Oréal — David has spent his career at the intersection of strategy, transformation, and human experience. He doesn't just study change. He builds it.
