Meet Your Host

Not Your Typical Host

D
David Bernardo Santo
CEO, LITS Adventures  ·  Host, LITS X
Forbes USA Expert Top 10 Innovator Portugal 40 Under 40 ×2 Nova SBE Faculty

Advisor to Amazon, Walmart, Sony Music, L’Oréal, and more. Building at the intersection of strategy, transformation, and human experience.

David Bernardo Santo is not your typical podcast host. Recognised by Forbes USA as one of the top experts in ecommerce and digital strategy in Latin America and named by Entrepreneur magazine as one of the top 10 innovators in Mexico, David has twice been awarded Portugal’s prestigious “40 Under 40.” He brings global experience, an entrepreneurial edge, and genuine curiosity to every conversation.

CEO and founder of LITS Adventures, advisor to companies like Amazon, Walmart, Sony Music, and L’Oréal, and the mind behind Catrina, a venture reimagining the funeral industry worldwide, David has spent his career at the intersection of strategy, transformation, and human experience. He doesn’t just study change. He builds it.

LITS X started as a project with his students at Nova School of Business & Economics and has grown into something much bigger: a long-format podcast where David and his students ask the questions most people are too polished to ask, covering everything from longevity and AI to death, work, and the stuff we’re all pretending not to worry about.

“Life Is Too Short not to make the best of it.”
